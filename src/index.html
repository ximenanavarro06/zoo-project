Become a Junior Zookeeper!

Learn & search for your favorite animals during your visit!

Are you ready to become a Junior ZooKeeper? Enter your name below to get started!

Step 1: Scroll to see the animals you need to find during your visit.

Step 2: Double click on the animal picture to learn more about the animals.

Step 3: Double click on the Animal Details to return to the animal pictures.

Step 4: Explore the zoo and keep a lookout for the animals.

Step 5: Once you spot the animal, click the "I SPOTTED THIS ANIMAL!" button.

Step 6: Try to spot all 10 animals during your zoo visit!

And don't forget to donate to your favorite animals!
